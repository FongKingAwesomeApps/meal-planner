Help
How do I pick meals? +

Tap the Pick tab, select your meals, then tap Send. The coordinator gets notified and adds them to the week plan.

Why are some meals locked? +

You've hit your weekly limit for that meal type. For example, if you've already picked your maximum Indulgent meal, all other Indulgent meals lock. Deselect one to swap.

How do I suggest a new meal? +

Tap the Suggest tab. You can describe a meal or paste a link from YouTube, AllRecipes, or anywhere. The coordinator reviews it and can build it into the recipe library.

How do I see what's for dinner? +

Tap the Plan tab to see the full week's meal plan. Tap any meal to see the recipe.
