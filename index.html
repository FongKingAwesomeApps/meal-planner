Help & FAQ
Getting started
How does this app work? +

There are two apps. This coordinator is for whoever plans the meals — it's where you select meals, build the shopping list, assign meals to each day of the week, and approve picks from the family. The family app is a simpler version that Seth, Jared and guests use to pick their favourite meals for the week.

How do I set up sync so everyone sees the same plan? +

Go to Settings → Family Links & Sync. Copy the coordinator link and bookmark it — that's your permanent link. Copy the family app link and send it to Seth, Jared and anyone else who picks meals. Anyone who opens those links is automatically connected to the same family data.

How do I add this to my home screen? +

On iPhone: open your coordinator link in Safari, tap the Share button (the box with an arrow pointing up), then tap "Add to Home Screen". Give it a name and tap Add. It'll launch full-screen like a native app. Make sure you use your coordinator link (with ?fid= in it) not just the base URL.

Meal ratings
What do Light, Balanced, and Indulgent mean? +

Every meal is rated based on five nutritional factors: sodium, saturated fat, fibre, protein, and sugar. Light meals hit most targets — lower sodium and fat, higher fibre and protein. Balanced meals are satisfying family dinners that are moderate on everything. Indulgent meals are the treats — higher fat, sodium, or sugar. Nothing is banned, it's about the week's overall balance.

What's the ideal weekly balance? +

Aim for 3–4 Light meals, 2–3 Balanced, and 1–2 Indulgent per week. The balance bar on the home screen updates as you plan — green means you're in a good range, orange means you might want to swap something out. These are guidelines, not rules.

Family picks
How do Seth and Jared send their picks? +

They open the family app link on their phone, tap their name, and pick their meals. Each person has a weekly allowance — for example, 3 picks with a maximum of 1 Indulgent meal. Once they've made their picks and hit the minimum Light requirement, they tap Send. Their picks arrive on your coordinator immediately (if sync is set up) or via a shared link.

Why are some meals greyed out in the family app? +

Greyed-out meals with a 🔒 are locked because selecting them would exceed the person's weekly allowance. For example, if Seth has already picked his one Indulgent meal for the week, all remaining Indulgent meals lock automatically. He'd need to deselect one to swap it.

How do I change someone's pick limits? +

Go to Settings → tap Edit next to the person's name. Use the + and - buttons to set how many total picks they get per week, the maximum number of Indulgent picks allowed, and the minimum Light picks required before they can send. Changes take effect immediately.

Meals & recipes
How do I add a new meal? +

Settings → Add Meal to Library. Type the meal name, pick a category, and tap ✨ Generate with AI. Claude will fill in the ingredients with quantities, step-by-step instructions with cook times, and a health rating. Review everything, edit anything that looks off, and tap Save. The meal appears in the picker immediately.

How do the kids suggest meals? +

In the family app, there's a Suggest tab (💡). They can either type a description ("that Korean chicken thing from Connor's") or paste a link from YouTube, AllRecipes, or anywhere else. It gets sent to the coordinator where you can review it and tap "Build with AI" to turn it into a proper recipe entry.

Shopping & planning
How does the shopping list work? +

Select your meals in the Meals tab, then tap Build Shopping List. Every ingredient from every selected meal is combined and sorted into Fridge and Pantry sections, with quantities shown. If the same ingredient appears in multiple meals the count shows (×2, ×3). Tap any item to check it off as you shop. Tap Share List to send it to yourself via iMessage or Notes.

Does the app work without internet? +

Yes. After the first load, the service worker caches the entire app. You can browse meals, view recipes, build shopping lists, and manage the plan with no connection. Any changes you make while offline will sync to the server next time you're online.

How do I back up my data? +

Settings → Backup & Restore → Create Backup. This generates a code that contains your family member settings and any custom meals you've added. Save it in Notes or email it to yourself. To restore on a new device, paste the code into the Restore field and tap Restore.
